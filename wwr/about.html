Learn About Us!

At Hyacinth White Water Rafting, we are passionate about delivering unforgettable outdoor experiences. Our mission is to connect adventurers with the beauty and excitement of nature through safe, thrilling, and eco-friendly rafting trips.

History

Founded in 2025, Hyacinth White Water Rafting began as a small family venture in Awka. Passionate about adventure, we’ve grown into a premier outfitter, offering thrilling, eco-friendly rafting trips while prioritizing safety and unforgettable experiences for all adventurers.

From beginner-friendly floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids, we offer a variety of trips tailored to all skill levels. Enjoy guided tours, top-notch equipment, and breathtaking scenery on every journey.

Adventure Awaits You!

At Hyacinth White Water Rafting, adventurers tackle thrilling rapids, navigate serene river stretches, and soak in Idaho’s breathtaking scenery. From heart-pounding Class IV rapids to tranquil floats, every trip delivers unforgettable excitement, camaraderie, and connection with nature’s wild beauty.
