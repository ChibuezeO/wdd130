Our Featured Rafting Trips

Family Float Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this half-day trip on the Lower River offers gentle Class II-III rapids, warm water, and sandy beaches for swimming. Enjoy stunning canyon views and wildlife spotting with our expert guides.

Duration: 4 hours | Difficulty: Beginner | Price: N2,000/adult, N1,500/child (6-12)

Rapid Rush Challenge

Tackle the thrilling Class III-IV rapids of the Main River on this full-day adventure. Ideal for adrenaline seekers, this trip includes expert guidance, heart-pounding rapids, and breathtaking Anambra wilderness scenery.

Duration: 8 hours | Difficulty: Intermediate | Price: N2,200/adult

Wilderness Escape

Embark on a 3-day journey through the Frank Church Wilderness on the Middle Fork of the River. Experience Class III-IV rapids, hot springs, and camping under the stars for an unforgettable adventure.

Duration: 3 days | Difficulty: Advanced | Price: 7,000/adult
